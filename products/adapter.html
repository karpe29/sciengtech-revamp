Opto-Mechanics

Adapter

SKU: SET-ADAPTER

Standard outer diameter: Ø25.4 mm · Two nylon-tipped setscrews for secure, damage-free clamping

The Laser / Fiber Collimator Adapter is designed to securely mount cylindrical laser sources and fiber collimators into standard Ø25.4 mm optical mounts, enabling precise alignment in optical and optomechanical setups. The adapter features two nylon-tipped setscrews that provide firm clamping while preventing scratches or damage to the component surface.

With its robust design and easy adjustment mechanism, the adapter simplifies laser alignment and positioning, making it ideal for research laboratories, educational institutions, and industrial optical systems.

Features

  • Standard outer diameter: Ø25.4 mm
  • Two nylon-tipped setscrews for secure, damage-free clamping
  • Enables precise alignment of lasers and optical components
  • Compatible with standard 1" optical mounts
  • Durable and precision-machined construction
  • Adapter helps to hold the laser to align with optical mount. With the help of adapter one can easily align the Laser without any difficult. To ease it 2 nylon tipped setscrews are provided. It helps to hold the collimator/Laser without damaging the surface.
