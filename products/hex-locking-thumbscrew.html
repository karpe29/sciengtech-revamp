SciEngTech Hex Locking Thumbscrew provide secure and precise locking of optical posts in optomechanical assemblies. Spring-loaded ball provides sufficient holding force during alignment, allowing fine positional adjustments before final locking.

The thumbscrew can be securely tightened using a standard 5 mm hex key, ensuring a rigid and stable connection for demanding optical applications. Compatible with standard Ø12.7 mm (1/2") post holders, it is an ideal accessory for optical breadboards, mounting systems, and precision alignment setups.

Material: Anodized Aluminum Designed and manufactured in India

Applications

  • Optical post holders and mounts
  • Precision alignment systems
  • Laser and photonics setups
  • Research and educational laboratories
