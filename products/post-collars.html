Post Collars

SKU: SET-PC

Slip-on design for Ø12.7 mm (1/2") optical posts · Restricts post movement and maintains stable positioning

SciEngTech Solutions offers precision-machined post collars designed for secure positioning and height control of Ø12.7 mm (1/2") optical posts. Manufactured from high-quality aluminum alloy and finished with a black anodized coating, these collars provide a durable, low-reflectivity solution for optical and optomechanical setups. It allows easy installation, removal, and repositioning of posts while maintaining accurate height settings in optical and optomechanical assemblies.

Features

  • Slip-on design for Ø12.7 mm (1/2") optical posts
  • Restricts post movement and maintains stable positioning
  • Fine height adjustment setscrew for precise positioning
  • Easy mounting and adjustment using the M4 knob
  • Black anodized finish for durability and reduced reflections
  • Precision-machined aluminium alloy construction
  • Manufactured in India

Applications

  • Optical and photonics laboratories
  • Laser alignment setups
  • Research and educational experiments
  • Precision optomechanical assemblies
  • Quantum optics and spectroscopy systems
