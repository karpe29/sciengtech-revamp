The table clamp features two cap screws: one engages an M6 tapped hole to allow height adjustment, enabling the clamp to accommodate a wide range of setups, while the second passes through a slotted hole to securely fasten the clamp to the optical table. Its compact design minimizes the footprint, making it suitable for space-constrained configurations.

The maximum thickness of optomechanical components that can be secured with the clamp depends on the length of the screws used for fastening.
