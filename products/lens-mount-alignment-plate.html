Lens Mount Alignment Plate

Optical Axis Alignment Plate for Lens Mounts

SKU: SET-LMAP

Removable alignment plate for convenient use during setup and alignment · Designed to assist laser beam alignment with pre-mounted lens holders

The alignment plate serves as a temporary reference tool during optical alignment procedures. It helps in defining the optical axis and ensures that the laser beam is properly centered through the lens system before final locking and operation.

It provides a reference surface to ensure accurate beam positioning and simplifies alignment procedures in complex optical systems.

Once alignment is completed, the plate can be removed without disturbing the setup.

Features

  • Removable alignment plate for convenient use during setup and alignment
  • Designed to assist laser beam alignment with pre-mounted lens holders
  • Ensures accurate beam positioning along the optical axis
  • Compatible with standard opto-mechanical lens mounts
  • Compact and easy-to-handle design for laboratory use
  • Precision-machined aluminium construction with black anodized finish

Applications

  • Laser beam alignment in optical benches
  • Lens system centering and calibration
  • Interferometer setup alignment
  • Educational optics experiments
  • Research laboratory optical assembly
  • Photonics and laser system alignment
