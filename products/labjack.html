Labjack

SKU: SET-LBJ-26

Compatible with Breadboards and Components · Vertical Travel: 26.5 mm

This lab jack offers a highly rigid design in a compact form factor. The precision adjustment mechanism ensures smooth vertical motion while supporting substantial loads.

Both the top and bottom plates include multiple tapped holes and counterbores, providing excellent flexibility for mounting various components.

Features

  • Vertical Travel: 26.5 mm
  • Minimum Height: 21.3 mm
  • Maximum Height: 47.8 mm
  • Mounting Surface: 76.2 mm × 101.6 mm
