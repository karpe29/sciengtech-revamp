SciEngTech Solutions offers high-quality pedestal base adapters from stainless steel for stable and reliable mounting of pedestal-style post holders in optical and optomechanical systems. Designed to interface seamlessly with SciEngTech pedestal forks, these adapters provide a foundation for precision alignment and positioning applications.

Available in both magnetic and non-magnetic versions, the adapters feature excellent surface finishing and are engineered for long-term durability in laboratory, industrial, and research environments. The integrated M6 × 20 threaded stud allows secure attachment to Ø12.7 mm (1/2") post holders.
