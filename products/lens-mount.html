Lens Mount

Lens Holder · Optical Lens Mount · Fixed Lens Holder · Lens Housing · OptiCell Lens Mount

SKU: SET-LMR-25

Designed for Ø25.4 mm (1") optics · Accommodates optics up to 7 mm thickness

SciEngTech Solutions Lens mounts are designed for securely holding and accurate positioning of Ø25.4 mm (1") optical components in optical and optomechanical systems. The mount features an internally threaded SMA (1.035"-40) interface and a compatible retaining ring for reliable optic retention while maintaining easy access for installation and replacement.

An M4 threaded mounting hole at the base enables direct attachment to Ø12.7 mm (1/2") optical posts, allowing seamless integration into standard laboratory and research setups. Manufactured from high-quality aluminum with a hard black anodized finish, the mount provides excellent durability, corrosion resistance, and reduced surface reflections.
