Collars

SKU: SET-PC

Designed for Ø12.7 mm (1/2") optical posts · Slip-on collar design for quick installation and adjustment

SciEngTech Post Collars provide a simple, reliable, and cost-effective solution for maintaining accurate post positioning and improving the stability of optical experiments.

SciEngTech Solutions offers high-quality post collars designed for secure positioning and height adjustment of Ø12.7 mm (1/2") optical posts. Manufactured from precision-machined aluminum alloy and finished with a durable black anodized coating, these collars provide a reliable and convenient method for supporting and positioning optical components in laboratory and research environments.

The slip-on design allows quick installation and removal, while the integrated M4 clamping knob ensures secure locking at the desired position. A fine adjustment setscrew enables precise height positioning and helps prevent unwanted post movement, improving the stability and repeatability of optical setups.
