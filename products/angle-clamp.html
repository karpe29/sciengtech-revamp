Angle Clamp

Rigid Assembly Clamps

SKU: SET-ANGLECLAMP

Compatible with Ø12.7 mm (1/2") optical posts · Ideal for building rigid and modular optomechanical structures

SciEngTech Rigid Assembly Clamps provide a simple and reliable solution for creating stable, repeatable, and highly rigid optomechanical assemblies using standard Ø12.7 mm optical posts.

Featuring double-bored through holes, the clamps provide enhanced mechanical stability through two-line contact with the posts, ensuring improved alignment accuracy, rigidity, and repeatability in demanding optical and photonics applications.

  • Ideal for building rigid and modular optomechanical structures
