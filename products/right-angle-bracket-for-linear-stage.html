Enables mounting of translation stages in the XZ or XYZ plane Six dowel pin holes ensure precise orthogonality and repeatable alignment Dimensions (L × W × H): 108 mm × 88 mm × 134 mm This component is an anodized aluminum right-angle bracket designed to orient translation stages vertically. It allows users to construct XZ and XYZ positioning systems, expanding standard horizontal configurations into multi-axis setups.

The base plate features six counterbored holes for M6 cap screws, enabling direct mounting onto an optical table. The vertical face includes: Two M6 tapped holes Eight 7.0 mm through holes Dowel pins are included with the base plate, adapter plate, and bracket to ensure accurate alignment and maintain orthogonality between mounted stages.
