The Base Plate is an adapter designed for PT Series translation stages, enabling the construction of XY and XYZ stage configurations. It is specifically intended for optical breadboard mounting, with four M6 mounting slots on the sides that allow obstruction-free installation when central counterbored holes are inaccessible. Additionally, it supports compatibility with right-angle brackets for vertical mounting, making it suitable for both horizontal and vertical setups.

This makes it ideal for XY configurations or already assembled XYZ multi-axis systems where the standard mounting points are obstructed. The base plate includes two alignment dowel pins and two M6 cap screws to ensure precise positioning and secure installation. It also allows stages with different travel ranges to be easily integrated within the same mechanical system.
