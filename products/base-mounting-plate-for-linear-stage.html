. The base plate provides additional mounting flexibility while offering extra clearance between the translation stage and the work surface. Counterbored clearance holes align with the tapped holes on the base of the stage, allowing mounting in two orthogonal orientations for versatile configuration.

This plate is compatible with metric versions of the stage. It measures 65.3 mm × 90.5 mm × 10 mm (L × W × H) and ships with two dowel pins. Additionally, it features four alignment holes to ensure precise positioning and maintain mounting orthogonality.
