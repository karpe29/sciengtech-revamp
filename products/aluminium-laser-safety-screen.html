SciEngTech Solutions offers high-quality Laser Safety Screens designed to minimize the transmission of stray and unwanted light into or out of sensitive optical experiments. These screens help improve experimental accuracy by reducing background reflections and isolating optical paths within laboratory setups.

Manufactured from aluminum and finished with a durable black anodized coating, the screens provide excellent light absorption, corrosion resistance, and long-term durability. Available in both flat and curved configurations, they can be easily positioned around optical assemblies to create effective light barriers and beam shields.

Integrated magnets at the base allow convenient placement on optical tables and breadboards, while a central slotted mounting hole enables secure installation on non-magnetic surfaces.

Applications

  • Laser and photonics laboratories
  • Quantum optics and spectroscopy experiments
  • Optical alignment and beam steering setups
  • Light-sensitive measurement systems
  • Educational and research laboratories
  • General optomechanical and laser-based experiments
