Opto-Mechanics

Universal Base Plate

SKU: SET-UNIVERSALBAS

Enables flexible mounting in any orientation · Central counterbored slot for continuous adjustment

The SET-UBP Series Universal Base Plates enhance the flexibility of optical setups by providing multi-standard mounting options in a compact form factor. Designed to overcome the limitations of standard 25 mm hole patterns, these plates allow components and stages to be mounted in arbitrary orientations and positions.

Each base plate features a central counterbored slot for continuous positional adjustment and corner mounting holes for secure attachment to optical tables or breadboards.

Features

  • Enables flexible mounting in any orientation
  • Central counterbored slot for continuous adjustment
  • Multiple thread standards (metric and imperial)
  • Corner counterbored holes for rigid mounting
  • Compatible with standard optical tables and breadboards
  • Supports integration with cage systems
  • Compact, rigid, and versatile design
  • Design & Functionality
  • The universal base plate provides a versatile mounting interface by combining slotted adjustment with multiple threaded hole patterns. This allows users to mount components that do not conform to standard grid spacing, making it ideal for custom or complex optomechanical assemblies.

Applications

  • Mounting of optical components outside standard hole patterns
  • Integration of mixed metric and imperial hardware
  • Optical breadboard prototyping
  • Multi-axis and custom optomechanical assemblies
  • Research and educational setups
  • Notes
  • Ensure compatibility of fasteners with selected thread standards
  • Use appropriate mounting torque for stable positioning
  • Central slot allows continuous rotational and translational adjustment
