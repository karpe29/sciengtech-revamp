BasePlate

SKU: SET-PB-H

Precision-machined aluminium alloy construction · Black anodized finish for durability and low reflectivity

SciEngTech Solutions offers high-quality optical post bases manufactured from durable aluminium alloy for secure mounting of Ø12.7 mm (1/2") posts and post holders on optical breadboards. Precision-machined and black anodized for enhanced durability and reduced light reflections, these post bases provide a stable foundation for optical and optomechanical assemblies.

Designed with M6 mounting slots, the bases can be easily positioned and secured on optical breadboards, enabling flexible setup configurations. Available in U-shaped, H-shaped, and square designs, they accommodate a wide range of mounting requirements in research, educational, and industrial applications.
