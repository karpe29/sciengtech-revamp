30 mm Cage System Alignment Plate

SKU: SET-CSAP

Designed for compatibility with 30 mm cage systems · Used for alignment of visible laser beams in cage-based setups

The alignment plate acts as a reference tool during the setup of cage systems. It helps in aligning and centering the laser beam through the optical axis, ensuring proper system configuration before final assembly. It is particularly useful during initial alignment and system calibration stages.

The Cage Plate Alignment Plate is designed for use with 30 mm cage system assemblies. It serves as a precision alignment accessory for accurately setting and verifying the path of visible laser beams within cage-based optical systems.

Features

  • Designed for compatibility with 30 mm cage systems
  • Used for alignment of visible laser beams in cage-based setups
  • Ensures accurate beam centering and optical axis alignment
  • Precision-machined construction for stable positioning
  • Compact and easy-to-integrate design
  • Suitable for laboratory optical assemblies

Applications

  • 30 mm cage system alignment and setup
  • Laser beam alignment in optical assemblies
  • Educational optics experiments
  • Photonics and laser laboratory setups
  • Interferometry and optical instrumentation alignment
