Cube Beamsplitter Mount

Optical Cube Mount · Beamsplitter Cube Holder · Cube Optics Mount

SKU: SET-CBM-25

Compatible with 25.4 mm (1") and 20.0 mm cube optics · Suitable for beamsplitter cubes, right-angle prisms, and similar optical components

SciEngTech Solutions offers cube beamsplitter mounts designed to securely hold cube-shaped optical components such as beamsplitter cubes and right-angle prisms. Compatible with both 25.4 mm (1") and 20.0 mm cube optics, these mounts provide a stable and convenient platform for integrating cube optics into optical and optomechanical systems.

The open-frame design allows secure mounting while minimizing direct contact with optical surfaces, helping to protect delicate optics from damage and contamination during installation and experimentation. The mount is compatible with standard cage systems and can accommodate various cube optics using appropriate adapters.

For easy integration into laboratory setups, the base incorporates an M4 threaded mounting hole, allowing direct attachment to Ø12.7 mm (1/2") optical posts.
