We offer a comprehensive range of diode lasers specifically designed for precision alignment applications, available across multiple wavelengths including 450 nm (blue), 520 nm (green), 780 nm, 850 nm, 940 nm, and 1050 nm (near-infrared). These laser modules are offered in low-power options of 1 mW and 5 mW, making them ideal for sensitive optical setups where stability and accuracy are critical. Our alignment lasers are well-suited for laboratory, research, and industrial use, ensuring reliable beam quality and consistent performance. Additionally, red and green laser modules are available with TTL modulation capabilities (0–10 kHz), enabling controlled operation for dynamic alignment and integration into automated systems.
