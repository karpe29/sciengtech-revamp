Flip Mount Adapter

Beam Diversion Mount · Beam Redirect Mount · FlipAlign Mount

SKU: SET-FMA-090-M

Suitable for 20 mm to 25 mm mirror mounts · Precision angular adjustment in two axes

SciEngTech Solutions offers precision-engineered Flip mount adapter designed for accurate angular alignment and stable positioning of mirrors and other optical components in laser and photonics applications. The mount provides smooth and repeatable adjustment in both axes, making it ideal for demanding laboratory and research environments.

Three mounting holes on the top surface allow flexible attachment and alignment of optomechanical components according to experimental requirements. The mount supports mirror holders in the 20 mm to 25 mm range and features a 90° rotation capability for convenient beam-path clearance and system integration.

Manufactured for high mechanical stability and long-term reliability, the mount delivers precise beam steering with excellent repeatability.
