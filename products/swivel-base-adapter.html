The 360° Swivel Base Adapter is thoughtfully engineered for seamless integration with standard Ø1/2" post holders featuring 1/4"-20 (M6) mounting holes. Designed for laboratories and advanced optical systems where precision and efficiency are paramount, it delivers smooth, controlled 360° rotational positioning for effortless alignment.

When combined with our Ø1/2" post holders, the adapter offers performance comparable to our renowned Universal Post Holders, providing elegant versatility within a streamlined footprint.

Crafted from precision-machined aluminium and finished in durable black anodisation, it reflects both mechanical excellence and aesthetic refinement.
