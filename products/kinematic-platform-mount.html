Kinematic Platform Mount

Precision Optic Positioning Platform · Kinematic Alignment Base · Precision Optomechanical Platform

SKU: SET-KPM-050

Suitable for mounting prisms, beamsplitters, waveplates, sensors, crystals, and other optical components · Compatible with both vertical and horizontal mounting configurations

Designed for laboratories requiring precision alignment, mechanical stability, and flexible integration with modern optical setups.

Our Kinematic Platform and Prism Mounts provide stable, high-precision positioning for standard and complex optical components. Designed for both vertical and horizontal mounting orientations, these mounts maintain a clear and unobstructed beam path while offering excellent mechanical stability.

Ideal for mounting prisms, beamsplitters, waveplates, sensors, crystals, and other optical components, the mounts are precision-machined from black anodized aluminium, ensuring structural rigidity, durability, and reliable performance in laboratory and research environments.

Features

  • Suitable for mounting prisms, beamsplitters, waveplates, sensors, crystals, and other optical components
  • Compatible with both vertical and horizontal mounting configurations
  • Maintains a clear and unobstructed optical beam path
  • Precision-machined black anodized aluminium construction
  • High mechanical stability and repeatable positioning
  • Precision Adjustment
  • Two-axis kinematic adjustment for precise angular alignment
  • Left- or right-handed configuration through platform repositioning
  • Fine-thread adjustment screws for smooth and highly sensitive control
  • Excellent repeatability for precision optical alignment and tuning
  • Mounting and Integration Features
  • Compatible with standard clamping arms
  • Mounting platform includes 4-40, M3, and M4 tapped holes
  • Multiple M4 tapped holes on the platform for versatile component mounting
  • Two Ø4.8 mm clearance holes for additional mounting flexibility
  • Through holes or tapped holes provided on the back plate for post mounting
  • M4 counterbore for secure post attachment
  • Black anodized finish minimizes stray reflections and enhances durability

Applications

  • Prism and beamsplitter alignment
  • Optical sensor positioning
  • Crystal and waveplate mounting
  • Laser beam steering and optical path adjustment
  • Quantum optics and photonics experiments
  • Educational, research, and industrial optical systems
