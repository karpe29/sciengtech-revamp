SciEngTech Solutions offers precision-machined optical post holders designed for secure mounting and accurate positioning of Ø12.7 mm (1/2") optical posts. Manufactured from high-quality Aluminium Alloy 6061 (Al6061) and finished with a durable hard black anodized coating, these post holders provide excellent stability, corrosion resistance, and low surface reflectivity for optical and photonics applications.

Available in various lengths from 34.7 mm to 104.7 mm, the post holders feature a spring-loaded clamping mechanism that enables smooth post movement during alignment and firm, repeatable locking once the desired position is achieved. M6 mounting hole at the Swivel base adaptre allows direct attachment to optical breadboards and mounting platforms.
