Magnetic Beam Height Ruler

SKU: SET-MBHR-6

Two magnets at base for positioning on optical breadboard. · Slotted hole in the centre for non magnetic surface mounting.

The Magnetic Beam Height Ruler is designed for precise laser beam height measurement and alignment in optical setups. Laser-engraved metric graduations, integrated beam pass-through holes, and a magnetic base, it enables quick and accurate alignment on optical breadboards and tables.

Its design allows easy use in optomechanical assemblies, making it an essential tool for alignment and diagnostics.

The base of the screen has magnets mounted on it for smooth positioning on the optical breadboard.

Features

  • Two magnets at base for positioning on optical breadboard.
  • Slotted hole in the centre for non magnetic surface mounting.
  • 1.0 mm metric graduations for precise measurement
  • Measures beam heights up to 6" (155 mm) or 12" (305 mm)
  • Finished with black anodization for durability and uniform diffusion
  • Ø2.0 mm alignment holes for beam transmission
