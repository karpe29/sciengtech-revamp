SciEngTech Solutions offers high-quality pedestal forks precision-machined from corrosion-resistant stainless steel for stable and flexible mounting of pedestal-style post holders in optical and optomechanical setups. Designed for use with Ø12.7 mm (1/2") posts and post holders, these forks provide a rigid yet adjustable support structure for accurate component positioning.

The swivel design allows 360° rotational adjustment, enabling precise placement and alignment of optical components without disturbing the rest of the experimental setup. With 12 mm thick construction and an M6 counterbored mounting slot, the pedestal fork ensures excellent stability and secure attachment to optical breadboards.
