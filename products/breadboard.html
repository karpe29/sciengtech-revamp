SciEngTech Solutions offers precision-engineered optical breadboards designed for stable mounting and alignment of optomechanical components and assemblies. Featuring a metric M6 tapped hole grid, these breadboards provide a versatile platform for building, testing, and prototyping optical, photonic, and laser-based systems.

Manufactured from high-quality aluminum with a hard black anodized finish, the breadboards offer excellent durability, corrosion resistance, and low surface reflectivity, making them ideal for laboratory and research environments.

Features

  • Precision M6 tapped metric hole grid for flexible component mounting
  • Thickness: 12.7 mm (1/2")
  • High-quality aluminum construction
  • Hard black anodized finish for durability and reduced reflections
  • Lightweight yet rigid design
  • Suitable for mounting a wide range of optomechanical components
  • Designed and manufactured in India

Applications

  • Optical system prototyping
  • Photonics research setups
  • Laser alignment platforms
  • Educational and research laboratory experiments
  • Mounting opto-mechanical subassemblies
