Viewing Screen

White viewing surface for enhanced visibility of interference patterns · Ideal for observing laser spots, fringes, and beam profiles

Designed for laboratories requiring a simple, reliable, and easy-to-integrate solution for visualizing optical patterns and interference fringes.

The SciEngTech Viewing Screen is designed for clear observation of interference patterns and optical beam profiles in educational, research, and laboratory experiments. Its matte white surface provides excellent contrast and visibility.

Manufactured from high-quality Perspex, the screen offers durability, lightweight handling, and consistent optical performance.

  • White viewing surface for enhanced visibility of interference patterns
  • Ideal for observing laser spots, fringes, and beam profiles
  • Manufactured from high-quality Perspex
  • M4 tapped hole at the bottom for convenient mounting
  • Compatible with standard opto-mechanical components and post assemblies
  • Lightweight and durable construction
  • Suitable for educational, research, and industrial optical setups

Applications

  • Optical alignment and beam visualization
  • Laser beam profiling
  • Quantum optics demonstrations
