Fiber Optics Collimator

SKU: SET-FIBEROPTICSC

SMA905 / FC-PC / FC-APC connector. · Easy to couple with any standard fiber optics cable.

Fiber optic collimators are used to couple light into and out of optical fibers. Fiber optics collimators have a SMA905, FC/PC, or FC/APC terminated optical fiber at one end and a focusing lens assembly at the other end.

The focusing lens assembly collimate the laser light onto the fiber connected at the FC/SMA end optical fiber. 190 nm to 4.5 µm wavelength selectivity.

Features

  • SMA905 / FC-PC / FC-APC connector.
  • Easy to couple with any standard fiber optics cable.
  • Compatible to multi-mode and single mode fiber.
  • Compatible up to 600 µm optical fiber.
  • Clear aperture of 6.0 mm.
  • 190 nm to 4.5 µm Wavelength Selectivity.
  • Laser Line Wavelength Selection.
  • Multiple AR lens coating options.
  • Multiple lens substrate options.
  • Stainless steel housing.
