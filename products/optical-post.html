Optical Post

SKU: SET-OPTICALPOST

7 configurations available · SKU from SET-P-20

SciEngTech Solutions offers high-quality Ø12.7 mm (1/2") stainless steel optical posts designed for stable mounting and precise positioning of optical and optomechanical components. Manufactured from premium-grade stainless steel with excellent surface finishing, these posts provide exceptional rigidity, durability, and corrosion resistance for demanding laboratory and industrial applications.

Available in lengths ranging from 20 mm to 200 mm, the posts are compatible with SciEngTech and other standard post holders. Each post features an M4 threaded hole at the top and an M6 threaded hole at the bottom, enabling versatile mounting configurations. A cross-drilled hole is provided for convenient tightening and adjustment during installation.
