SciEngTech Solutions offers Ø1" (SM1-threaded) lens tubes for mounting, spacing, and protecting optical components in a wide range of optical and optomechanical assemblies. These lens tubes provide a convenient and modular solution for integrating lenses, filters, apertures, and other optical elements into laboratory and industrial setups.

Optical components can be securely positioned within the tube using compatible retaining rings, allowing multiple optics to be mounted at precise separations. The lens tubes are fully compatible with 30 mm cage systems, enabling the construction of rigid and customizable optical assemblies.

Features

  • Designed for mounting and housing optical components within a protected enclosure
  • Compatible with Ø1" (SM1-threaded) optics and accessories
  • Optics can be secured and spaced using compatible retaining rings
  • Precision-machined aluminum construction
  • Durable black anodized finish for reduced reflections and enhanced corrosion resistance
  • Fully stackable design for custom optical path lengths
  • Compatible with SM1-threaded lens tubes, adapters, and mounts
  • Seamlessly integrates with standard 30 mm cage systems
  • Lightweight, rigid, and easy to assemble

Applications

  • Lens and filter mounting
  • Beam expansion and focusing assemblies
  • Optical instrumentation and prototyping
  • Laser and photonics systems
  • Spectroscopy and imaging setups
  • Research, educational, and industrial optical experiments
